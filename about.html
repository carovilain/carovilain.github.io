About


Caro is a Visual Storyteller & Mobility Advocate based in Santa Monica, CA. Through her extensive knowledge of video editing and storytelling, she has worked on campaigns for clients including Paramount Studios, Netflix, DreamWorks, LA28, and Sony.

Living car-free in Los Angeles, she developed a passion for mobility advocacy and the many issues it intersects with. She now utilizes her skills to amplify the messages of local advocacy organizations such as Streets For All, Santa Monica's Big Blue Bus, Streets Are For Everyone, and MoveLA.

Her background in marketing has also allowed her to work on social media campaigns for brands whose mission aligns with her passion for safe streets, including Lumos helmets and Wombi.

Caro is determined to use her skills to promote messages of equity, accessibility, and inclusivity.
